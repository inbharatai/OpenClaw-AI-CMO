### Tier 2 — Ask Only If Truly Needed
- A required secret is genuinely missing after checking all approved sources
- A new domain or tool action is outside the allowlist

- A cost threshold is exceeded
- Production/security-sensitive action required

- Paid avatar video generation (HeyGen)
- Founder face or voice workflows
- High-cost media generation beyond budget
- New skill installs without founder approval
- Deletion/overwriting of critical production data
- Secret rotation unless instructed
- Major legal, political, or sensitive public actions
- Major security changes
