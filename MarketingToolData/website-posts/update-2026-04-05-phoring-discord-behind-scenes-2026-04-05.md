# Behind the Scenes: Building the Multi-Agent Knowledge Graph

Phoring is advancing its decision intelligence platform with a foundational multi-agent knowledge graph that transforms documents into collaborative simulations with source-cited forecasts.

## What's New
- **Graph Build**: Extracted entities and relationships from structured/unstructured documents
- **Environment Setup**: Configured simulation parameters for scenario analysis
- **Simulation**: Executed multi-agent scenario analysis with collaborative reasoning
- **Report Generation**: Created source-cited forecasts and evidence-backed insights
- **Interactive Q&A**: Enabled follow-up questions with traceable evidence trails

## Why It Matters
This development provides transparency into how Phoring's platform processes information, demonstrating the technical foundation for its decision intelligence capabilities. Users can now trace how insights are generated through explicit evidence chains.

Upcoming work will focus on expanding simulation capabilities and improving source citation accuracy.
