# New Feature: Smart Content Queue

We just shipped the Smart Content Queue for our AI workspace tools.

Key features:
- Automatic content routing to the right channel (website, social, newsletter)
- 4-level approval system: auto-approve for low-risk, score-gated for medium, human review for high-risk
- Rate limiting per channel to prevent over-posting
- Full audit trail with logs and evidence

This is a major milestone for solo builders who want AI-powered marketing without the manual overhead.

Built with local-first architecture, running on Ollama with qwen3:8b for content and qwen2.5-coder:7b for automation scripts.

Next up: Discord webhook integration and weekly roundup automation.
