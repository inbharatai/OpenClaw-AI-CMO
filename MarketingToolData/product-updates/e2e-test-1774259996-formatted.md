# OpenClaw v1.0 Launch

## Summary
We’re excited to launch OpenClaw, our AI CMO operating system that automates content creation, approval, and distribution across 12 channels. Built with local LLMs, it eliminates cloud dependency for businesses seeking full control over their content workflows.

## Changes
- Introduces OpenClaw as an AI-powered content management system
- Enables automated content generation, approval, and multi-channel distribution
- Utilizes local LLMs for processing, removing reliance on cloud infrastructure
- Supports 12 content distribution channels out of the box

## User Impact
Businesses managing content across multiple platforms will benefit from automated workflows that reduce manual effort and improve consistency without compromising data security.

## Technical Notes
The system leverages local LLMs for all content processing tasks, ensuring data remains on-premises. This architecture eliminates latency issues associated with cloud-based solutions while maintaining robust content curation capabilities.
