## Problem
Decision‑makers need fast, source‑cited forecasts across policy, market, and tech trends, but manual research is slow and error‑prone.

## Solution
**Phoring** turns any document (PDF, DOCX, web page) into a **knowledge graph** and runs multi‑agent simulations that output:
- Quantitative forecasts
- Confidence scores
- Full source citations

It supports **social‑simulation** on Twitter/Reddit, enabling “what‑if” scenario analysis for public opinion, product adoption, or policy impact.

## Proof Points
- **4 × speed‑up** vs. manual analyst research on comparable datasets.
- **100 %** source‑cited outputs (every claim links back to a document snippet).
- Successfully used in a pilot for **government‑scheme adoption modelling**.
