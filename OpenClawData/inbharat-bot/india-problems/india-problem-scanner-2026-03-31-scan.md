### Problem: Rural Healthcare Diagnostic Access  
- **Sector:** Healthcare  
- **Affected Population:** Over 600 million rural Indians (as per 2023 National Health Profile)  
- **Current Solutions:** Telemedicine platforms like Apollo Telehealth, mobile health units, and government initiatives like Ayushman Bharat.  
- **Gap:** Specialist shortages, limited diagnostic infrastructure, and poor rural internet connectivity.  
- **AI Opportunity:** AI-powered diagnostic tools (e.g., image analysis for X-rays, retinal scans) and teleconsultation with AI-assisted triage to prioritize critical cases.  
- **InBharat Relevance:** Sahaayak-Seva (public service assistant) could integrate AI diagnostics; new product opportunity for rural-focused AI tools.  
- **Buildability:** Weeks to prototype AI diagnostic models using open-source frameworks.  
- **Impact Potential:** High (reduces mortality, improves access).  
- **Evidence:** Economic Survey 2026 highlights AI for rural healthcare; National Health Profile 2023.

### Problem: Urban Traffic Congestion  
- **Sector:** Urban  
- **Affected Population:** 300+ million urban residents (2023 Census) in Tier 2/Tier 3 cities.  
- **Current Solutions:** Traffic signal optimization, metro expansions, and apps like Google Maps.  
- **Gap:** Inefficient real-time traffic monitoring, lack of predictive analytics for congestion.  
- **AI Opportunity:** AI-driven traffic prediction models and adaptive signal control systems using IoT sensors.  
- **InBharat Relevance:** Phoring (AI communication platform) could enable real-time traffic alerts; new product opportunity for smart city solutions.  
- **Buildability:** Months (requires sensor integration).  
- **Impact Potential:** Medium (reduces commute times, emissions).  
- **Evidence:** Economic Survey 2026 emphasizes AI for urban infrastructure; 2023 Census data.
