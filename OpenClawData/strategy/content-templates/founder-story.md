# Founder Story Template

## Objective
Share authentic founder perspective — what we're building, why, what we learned. Build trust through transparency.

## Hook Styles
- "I've been building {product/thing} for {timeframe}. Here's what I've learned."
- "Nobody tells you this about building AI products in India."
- "This week I shipped {thing}. It took {effort}. Here's the real story."
- "A mistake I made while building {product}. And how I fixed it."

## Structure
1. **Personal hook**: Authentic moment, challenge, or realization
2. **Context**: What you were building and why
3. **Insight**: The non-obvious thing you learned
4. **Connection**: Why this matters beyond just you
5. **CTA**: Follow the journey, check out what we're building

## Tone
- First person always
- Honest — include failures and pivots
- No humble-bragging
- No "10 lessons I learned" unless each lesson is genuinely valuable
- Builder perspective: we make things, we ship things, we learn things

## Image Style
- Behind-the-scenes (terminal screenshots, whiteboard, workspace)
- Product milestone screenshots
- Simple text card with the key insight
- NO: Staged "founder at desk" stock photos

## Safe Claim Rules
- Personal experiences are always safe to share
- Avoid implying scale or traction that doesn't exist
- "Building" is different from "launched and adopted"
