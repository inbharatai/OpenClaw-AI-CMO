# India-First Narrative Template

## Objective
Position InBharat products within the India-specific AI narrative. Why India needs its own AI tools, what's different about building for India, what "AI for Bharat" actually means.

## Hook Styles
- "India doesn't need another ChatGPT wrapper. It needs {specific thing}."
- "{Stat about India's digital landscape}. This is why we build differently."
- "Building AI for 1.4B people means {specific constraint/opportunity}."
- "What works in Silicon Valley doesn't work in {Indian context}."

## Structure
1. **India-specific problem or context**: Real, specific, not generic
2. **Why existing solutions don't fit**: Language, price, context gaps
3. **What we're building differently**: Specific approach, not vague "for India"
4. **Product connection**: Natural tie to InBharat product (not forced)
5. **Vision/CTA**: Where this is going, how to follow

## Tone
- Proud but not jingoistic
- Specific, not generic "India is amazing"
- Problem-first, not flag-first
- Data-informed when possible
- Builder perspective, not commentator

## Image Style
- India context visuals (real scenarios, not stock)
- Multilingual interface examples
- Rural + urban inclusion
- NO: Flag overlays, tricolor gradients, political imagery

## Safe Claim Rules
- India population/digital stats must be from verifiable sources
- No comparison claims ("India's best AI") without proof
- "India-first" is a design philosophy claim, not a ranking
