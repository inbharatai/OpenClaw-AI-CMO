# Product Introduction Template

## Objective
Introduce {product} to an audience that hasn't heard of it. Focus on the problem it solves, not the feature list.

## Hook Styles
- "Most people don't realize {problem}. That's why we built {product}."
- "{Audience} in India face {specific challenge}. Here's what we're building."
- "We asked: what if {capability} was accessible to everyone in India?"

## Structure
1. **Problem** (2-3 sentences): What real problem exists
2. **Solution** (1-2 sentences): What the product does, simply
3. **Differentiator** (1 sentence): Why this is different from alternatives
4. **Proof** (1 sentence): What exists today (live, in development, open source)
5. **CTA** (1 sentence): Single next step

## CTA Variants
- Visit {website_url}
- Follow our journey building it
- Star us on GitHub: {github_url}
- Join our community

## Image Style
- Product interface screenshot or clean mockup
- Problem-solution visual (before/after concept)
- Audience-in-context visual (Indian users, real scenarios)

## Safe Claim Rules
- Only use claims from product-truth/{product}.md Safe Claims section
- Status must match: "live" products can say "try it", "development" products say "we're building"
- Never claim user numbers, revenue, or partnerships without verification
