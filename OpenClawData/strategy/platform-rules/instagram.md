# Instagram — Platform Rules

## Content Types
- Reels (15-90 seconds) — PRIMARY format
- Carousel posts (up to 10 slides)
- Single image posts
- Stories (24-hour, behind-the-scenes)

## Tone
- Visual-first, punchy, engaging
- Hook in first 3 seconds (Reels) or first slide (carousel)
- Conversational but professional
- Use of trending audio where relevant

## Format Rules
- Vertical 9:16 for Reels
- Square 1:1 or 4:5 for feed posts
- Caption max: 2,200 characters (aim for 150-300)
- Hashtags: 5-15 relevant (never 30 spam hashtags)
- Always include CTA in caption

## Content Priorities
- Product demos and walkthroughs
- Behind-the-scenes of building
- AI explained simply (visual format)
- India problem → solution stories
- Founder journey moments
- Quick tip Reels

## Approval Level
- L2 (score-gated) for most content
- L3 (review required) for product claims, competitor mentions
- Auto-reject: unverified metrics, fake UI screenshots

## Posting Cadence
- Target: 1 post/day (Reel or carousel)
- Stories: 2-3/day when active
- Never: bulk posting, engagement bait

## Restrictions
- No fake engagement tactics
- No purchased followers/likes references
- No unverified before/after claims
- No competitor bashing
- Product screenshots must be real, current UI
