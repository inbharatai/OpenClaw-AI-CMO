# YouTube Shorts — Platform Rules

## Content Types
- Shorts (15-60 seconds) — vertical, mobile-first
- No long-form videos in this pipeline

## Tone
- Quick, punchy, informative
- Hook in first 2 seconds
- Educational or demo-driven
- Founder personality welcome

## Format Rules
- Vertical 9:16
- 15-60 seconds optimal
- Title: max 100 characters, front-load keywords
- Description: concise, include product links
- No end screens (Shorts don't support them well)

## Content Priorities
- Quick AI demos
- "Did you know" India AI facts
- Product feature highlights (30-second walkthrough)
- Code snippet explanations
- AI concept simplification
- Build log snippets

## Approval Level
- L2 (score-gated) for educational/demo content
- L3 (review required) for product claims, founder face content

## Posting Cadence
- Target: 3-5 Shorts/week
- Batch creation preferred (record 5, schedule across week)

## Restrictions
- No clickbait thumbnails with false promises
- No "subscribe for more" as main content
- No unverified performance claims
- No competitor name-dropping without context
