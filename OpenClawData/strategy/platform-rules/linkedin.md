# LinkedIn — Platform Rules

## Content Types
- Text posts (1,300 character optimal, 3,000 max)
- Document/carousel posts (PDF slides)
- Short articles
- Polls (sparingly)

## Tone
- Professional but not corporate
- Founder-authentic: real challenges, real learnings
- Thoughtful, data-aware, India-context-rich
- First-person narrative preferred
- No LinkedIn-bro style engagement bait

## Format Rules
- Hook in first 2 lines (before "see more" fold)
- Short paragraphs (1-2 sentences each)
- Use line breaks generously
- Emojis: 0-2 max, only if natural
- Hashtags: 3-5 relevant, at end

## Content Priorities
- Founder insights on building in India
- AI industry analysis (India-focused)
- Product milestone announcements
- Hiring/team updates when relevant
- Partnership and collaboration announcements
- Lessons learned from building

## Approval Level
- L2 for thought leadership and updates
- L3 for partnership announcements, hiring posts
- L4 (block) for funding claims, "better than X" posts

## Posting Cadence
- Target: 3-4 posts/week
- Best times: Tue-Thu, 8-10 AM IST

## Restrictions
- No engagement bait ("agree?", "thoughts?", "like if you...")
- No humble-bragging
- No unverified metrics
- No "we raised X" without actual announcement
- No fake vulnerability posts
- Every claim must be provable
