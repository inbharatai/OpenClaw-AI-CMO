# X — Platform Rules

## Content Types
- Single tweets (280 characters)
- Threads (3-7 tweets)
- Quote tweets with commentary
- Image + caption posts

## Tone
- Sharp, direct, opinionated
- India-AI perspective
- Builder credibility over marketing polish
- Concise — every word earns its place

## Format Rules
- Tweet: max 280 characters
- Thread: start with hook, number if >3 tweets
- Images: 16:9 or 1:1
- Links: use sparingly (algorithm deprioritizes)
- Hashtags: 0-2 only

## Content Priorities
- Hot takes on AI in India
- Product proof snippets (screenshots, demos)
- India problem observations
- Build updates (short, punchy)
- Retweet + comment on relevant AI/India news
- Thread breakdowns of complex topics

## Approval Level
- L2 for standard posts
- L3 for controversial takes, competitor mentions
- L4 for anything involving legal, funding, partnerships

## Posting Cadence
- Target: 1-2 tweets/day
- Threads: 1-2/week
- Engage with replies daily

## Restrictions
- No @ mentioning people without genuine context
- No ratio-seeking or controversy-farming
- No unverified claims
- No "thread 🧵" cliche openings
- No engagement bait polls
