# Content Buckets — Master List

Every piece of content must map to exactly one bucket. No off-bucket content.

## 1. Product Proof
Show the product working. Screenshots, demos, walkthroughs.
Platforms: Instagram, Shorts, LinkedIn, X

## 2. Feature Walkthrough
Explain a specific feature in depth. Tutorial style.
Platforms: Shorts, Instagram carousel, LinkedIn

## 3. Founder Insight
Personal perspective from building InBharat. Real, not performative.
Platforms: LinkedIn, X, Instagram Stories

## 4. AI Made Simple
Explain an AI concept in plain language. Educational.
Platforms: Shorts, Instagram Reel, X thread

## 5. AI for Bharat
India-specific AI applications, opportunities, and challenges.
Platforms: LinkedIn, X, Shorts

## 6. Multilingual AI
Content about or in multiple Indian languages.
Platforms: Instagram, Shorts, Discord

## 7. Behind the Scenes
Real footage/screenshots of building, debugging, launching.
Platforms: Instagram Stories, Discord, X

## 8. Launch / Update
New feature, new product, version release announcements.
Platforms: All (LinkedIn primary, Discord, X, Instagram)

## 9. Market Gap Insight
Analysis of where India's market needs AI solutions.
Platforms: LinkedIn, X thread

## 10. India Problem Insight
Real problems discovered through scanning India's landscape.
Platforms: LinkedIn, X, Shorts

## 11. Opportunity Insight
Government tenders, grants, programs, or corporate opportunities found.
Platforms: LinkedIn, X

## 12. Community Building
Engagement, discussion starters, community highlights.
Platforms: Discord, X, LinkedIn

## 13. Mission / Vision
InBharat's purpose, values, and long-term goals.
Platforms: LinkedIn, Instagram, Discord

## 14. Weekly Learnings
What was learned this week from building, scanning, outreach.
Platforms: LinkedIn, Discord, X thread

## 15. Build Log
What was built, fixed, shipped today/this week.
Platforms: Discord, X, Instagram Stories

## 16. Promotional Teaser
Upcoming launch or feature preview.
Platforms: Instagram, X, Discord

## 17. Ecosystem Correction
Correcting outdated info, updating public positioning, clarifying.
Platforms: LinkedIn, X

## 18. Under-Promoted Highlight
Surfacing an existing product/feature that deserves more attention.
Platforms: All
