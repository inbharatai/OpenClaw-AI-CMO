# OpenClaw — Product Truth

## One-Line Definition
Multi-channel AI gateway for content creation, approval, and distribution with built-in safety controls.

## Who It Serves
- Solo founders managing social media presence
- Small teams needing AI content operations
- Builders wanting automated but controlled content pipelines

## Top Use Cases
- Social media content generation and scheduling
- Multi-channel content distribution with approval gates
- AI-assisted copywriting with brand voice enforcement
- Content pipeline automation (daily/weekly/monthly)

## Strongest Differentiators
- 4-level approval system with risk scoring
- 14 channel support with per-channel guardrails
- Local-first AI (Ollama) with optional cloud escalation
- Shell-script infrastructure (transparent, auditable, hackable)
- 66+ skill templates for content variety
- Real safety controls (not just prompts)

## Safe Claims
- "Open source AI content operations tool"
- "Multi-channel content pipeline with approval controls"
- "Local-first AI with optional cloud models"
- "66+ content generation skills"
