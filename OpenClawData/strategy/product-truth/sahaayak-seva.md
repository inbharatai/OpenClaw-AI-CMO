# Sahaayak Seva — Product Truth

## One-Line Definition
AI-powered public service and welfare delivery assistant for Indian government schemes and citizen services.

## Who It Serves
- Citizens accessing government schemes (ICDS, PMJAY, MGNREGA, etc.)
- Anganwadi workers, ASHA workers, and frontline government staff
- Government departments seeking digital service delivery
- Rural and semi-urban populations

## Top Use Cases
- Government scheme information and eligibility checking
- Citizen service request handling
- Frontline worker task assistance
- Multilingual public service delivery

## Strongest Differentiators
- Deep knowledge of Indian government schemes
- Designed for low-connectivity, low-literacy contexts
- Multilingual including Hindi and regional languages
- Built for real government workflow integration

## Safe Claims
- "AI assistant for Indian government scheme access"
- "Designed for citizens and frontline workers"
- "Multilingual public service delivery"

## Restricted Claims
- No claims of government adoption without formal agreements
- No claims of deployment in specific states without evidence
- No citizen count claims without verification
- No accuracy claims for scheme eligibility without testing

- **Preferred colors**: #0A74DA (blue), #F5A623 (orange), #FFFFFF (white)
- **Visual mood**: Trustworthy, accessible, government-grade, inclusive
