# TestsPrep — Product Truth

## One-Line Definition
AI-powered test preparation platform for Indian competitive exams and assessments.

## Who It Serves
- Students preparing for competitive exams (UPSC, JEE, NEET, SSC, etc.)
- Educators creating assessment content
- Coaching institutes seeking AI augmentation

## Top Use Cases
- Practice question generation
- Performance analysis and weak area identification
- Study plan optimization
- Mock test creation

## Strongest Differentiators
- Built specifically for Indian exam patterns
- AI-generated practice aligned to syllabus
- Affordable compared to premium coaching

## Safe Claims
- "AI-powered test prep for Indian exams"
- "Practice questions aligned to Indian syllabus"

## Restricted Claims
- No "guaranteed results" or score improvement percentages without data
- No ranking claims without independent verification
- No comparison to coaching institutes without evidence

- **Preferred colors**: #0A74DA (blue), #10B981 (green), #F5A623 (orange)
- **Visual mood**: Serious, structured, achievement-oriented, modern education-tech
