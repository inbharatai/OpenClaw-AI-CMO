# UniAssist — Product Truth

## One-Line Definition
AI-powered university and higher education assistant for Indian students.

## Who It Serves
- College and university students in India
- Students applying to universities
- Academic advisors and counselors

## Top Use Cases
- University application guidance
- Course selection assistance
- Academic query resolution
- Career pathway exploration

## Strongest Differentiators
- India-specific university knowledge
- Practical guidance for Indian higher education system
- Multilingual support for regional language speakers

## Safe Claims
- "AI assistant for Indian university students"
- "Guidance for Indian higher education"

## Restricted Claims
- No admission success rate claims without data
- No specific university endorsements without verification

## Visual Identity
- **Preferred colors**: #0A74DA (blue), #6366F1 (indigo), #FFFFFF (white)
- **Visual mood**: Modern education-tech, aspirational, trustworthy, professional
- **Image types**: University campus contexts, career path diagrams, student guidance UI mockups
- **Image prompt rules**: Show aspirational but realistic Indian university contexts. Clean guidance interfaces. Career path visualizations. Modern education technology. No childish or generic campus imagery.
- **Prohibited**: Childish school cliches, graduation cap clipart, generic campus stock photos
