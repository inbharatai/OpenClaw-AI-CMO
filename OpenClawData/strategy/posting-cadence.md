# Posting Cadence

## Weekly Targets

| Platform | Posts/Week | Best Days | Best Times (IST) | Priority |
|----------|-----------|-----------|-------------------|----------|
| Instagram | 5-7 (Reels + carousel) | Daily | 12-2 PM, 7-9 PM | HIGH |
| YouTube Shorts | 3-5 | Tue, Thu, Sat | 4-6 PM | HIGH |
| LinkedIn | 3-4 | Tue, Wed, Thu | 8-10 AM | HIGH |
| X | 7-10 (tweets + threads) | Daily | 10 AM, 6 PM | HIGH |
| Discord | Daily engagement | Daily | Anytime | MEDIUM |

## Content Mix Per Week (Target)
- 2x Product Proof
- 2x AI Made Simple / AI for Bharat
- 1x Founder Insight
- 1x India Problem / Market Gap
- 1x Build Log / Behind the Scenes
- 1x Weekly Learnings (Friday/Saturday)
- 1x Launch/Update (when available)
- 2x Community engagement (Discord + X replies)

## Pipeline Schedule
- **Daily (6 AM):** Intake processing, content generation, approval routing
- **Weekly (Monday 8 AM):** Editorial calendar, weekly roundup, newsletter draft
- **Monthly (1st, 9 AM):** Pillar review, campaign theme, performance summary

## Batch Creation Strategy
- Record 5 Shorts in one session, schedule across week
- Write 3-4 LinkedIn posts on Sunday, schedule Tue-Thu
- Prepare Instagram carousels in advance, post daily
- X: mix of scheduled posts + real-time engagement
