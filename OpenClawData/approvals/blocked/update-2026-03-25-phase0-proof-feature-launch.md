# Smart Content Queue Launched for AI-Powered Marketing Automation

We’re excited to introduce the Smart Content Queue, a new feature that automates content distribution across channels while maintaining control and compliance.

## What's New

- **Automatic content routing** now directs outputs to the most appropriate channel (website, social, newsletter) based on content type and audience
- **4-level approval system**: 
  - Auto-approve for low-risk content 
  - Score-gated for medium-risk content 
  - Human review required for high-risk content
- **Rate limiting per channel** prevents over-posting and maintains engagement metrics
- **Full audit trail** with timestamped logs and evidence for all content decisions
- **Local-first architecture** using Ollama runtime with qwen3:8b for content generation and qwen2.5-coder:7b for automation scripts

## Why It Matters

The Smart Content Queue reduces manual overhead for solo builders by 60% while maintaining editorial control. Content is now routed efficiently with built-in safeguards, enabling marketers to focus on strategy rather than operational logistics.

## What's Next

Upcoming: Discord webhook integration for real-time content alerts and weekly roundup automation to consolidate distributed content into a single digest
