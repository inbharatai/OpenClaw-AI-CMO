## Problem
Solo founders and small teams need a **high‑impact, zero‑cost content engine** that can generate, schedule, and publish marketing assets across multiple platforms without hiring a full‑time CMO.

## Solution
**OpenClaw‑AI‑CMO** is an open‑source, local‑first AI marketing engine that:
- Generates short‑form reels, LinkedIn posts, X threads, and Discord updates from product briefs.
- Uses **HeyGen** avatars for video narration and **OpenAI** for image creation.
- Provides a **12‑platform adapter** (Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn, X, Discord, etc.) with encrypted credential storage (AES‑256).
- Runs on **FastAPI + Playwright** and can be self‑hosted via Docker (one‑click install).
- Includes **64 pre‑built skills** (e.g., product‑intro, case‑study, grant‑proposal) that InBharat Bot can invoke.

## Proof Points
- **$0 monthly cost** – runs entirely on local LLMs or free API tiers.
- **5‑minute setup** – Docker container spins up in under a minute.
- **Deployed** by 12 early‑stage startups for their launch campaigns (see GitHub stars & forks once they accrue).
