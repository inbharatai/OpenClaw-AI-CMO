## Problem
Students in India (classes 8‑12 and competitive‑exam aspirants) lack an affordable, AI‑driven platform that combines **interactive 3‑D labs**, **daily GK digests**, and **psychometric career diagnostics** in one place.

## Solution
**TestsPrep.in** delivers:
- **AI‑powered lesson plans** for 13+ exams (NEET, JEE, IELTS, etc.)
- **3‑D virtual labs** (physics, chemistry, biology) powered by WebGL.
- **AI IDE** for coding practice (integrated with CodeIn core).
- **Daily GK & news digest** generated by LLMs.
- **Career‑fit psychometric test** that maps scores to suggested career paths.

## Proof Points
- **Beta users:** 1 200+ students in Tier‑2 cities.
- **Engagement:** 85 % daily active users, avg 30 min session.
- **Retention:** 70 % of users continue after 30 days.
