**Instagram Reel Script for Promotion**

**Opening Scene (0-5 seconds)**
- Show the product in action with upbeat music.

**Voiceover (5-10 seconds)**
- "Introducing our top product of the week!"

**Main Features (10-45 seconds)**
- Highlight unique features (text overlays).
- Quick testimonials or quotes from satisfied customers.

**Call to Action (45-60 seconds)**
- Encourage viewers to check the link in bio.
- "Don’t miss out!" 

**Closing Scene (60 seconds)**
- Show the product logo and website link.
