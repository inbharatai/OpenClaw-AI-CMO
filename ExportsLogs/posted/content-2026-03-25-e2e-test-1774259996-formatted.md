# OpenClaw v1.0 Launch

We’re launching OpenClaw v1.0, our AI-powered content management system that automates content creation, approval, and distribution across 12 channels. Built with local LLMs, it gives businesses full control over content workflows without cloud dependency.

## What's New

- **Introduces OpenClaw as an AI CMO operating system** for automated content creation, approval, and multi-channel distribution
- **Supports 12 content channels out of the box** (e.g., LinkedIn, Twitter, email, etc.)
- **Processes all content locally** using on-premises LLMs, eliminating cloud infrastructure reliance
- **Streamlines workflows** with automated generation and approval processes

## Why It Matters

Businesses managing content across multiple platforms gain efficiency through automated workflows that reduce manual effort while maintaining data security and consistency across channels.
